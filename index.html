Ashok Singh Sairam

Prof., Department of Mathematics, IIT Guwahati

Ashok joined the department of Mathematics in August 2017 after working for eight and a half years in the department of Computer Science and Engineering at IIT Patna. His research interests are in the broad areas of computer networks and network security. Currently, he is working in software-defined networks, crowdsourcing techniques, and location-based privacy. He received his B.Tech degree in Computer Science from NIT Silchar and his M.Tech and Ph.D. degrees from IIT Guwahati.
